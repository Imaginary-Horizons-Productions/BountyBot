BountyBot tabulates user data as part of its nominal functions; recording XP, bounties, toasts, and seasonal participation are data that is associated with individual Discord accounts.

Imaginary Horizons Productions uses this data to investigate bugs and predict if a new feature or proposed feature change will have the desired impact. No effort is made on the part of Imaginary Horizons Productions to associate Discord accounts with preferences or associate Discord accounts with other forms of identification.

No data collected by BountyBot will ever be sold or given to external companies. However, BountyBot will show your account name details, account creation times, bounty history, and toast history to bot managers to aid with identification of burner accounts and other server moderation.

BountyBot will not begin tabulating data associated with your account until you interact with it in a way that requires data tabulation (eg post a bounty, have a toast raised to you, etc). If you leave a server with BountyBot, your data will be deleted. If you kick BountyBot from a server, all user data will be deleted.
